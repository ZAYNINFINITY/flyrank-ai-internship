# What Are You Proving? — My Proof Statement

**Week 1 · AI Fluency track**

## The statement

I build full-stack SaaS products for real businesses — POS-it is the proof,
an offline-first point-of-sale desktop app built for versatile small
businesses, with an actual pilot client running it day to day. I don't just
wire up whatever an AI hands me; I make the calls on auth, sessions, and API
design myself, which is exactly the stuff most AI-assisted developers skip
past. I'm using that as my foundation to move into application security
next. The person I want to reach is a small business or SaaS founder who
needs a full-stack developer they can actually trust with the parts of an
app that break silently in production — not just someone who makes the UI
look nice. What I want them to do: book a short call with me to talk about
a trial task or contract work.

## Why this needs to exist

A CV can say "problem solver," but it can't show that I spent hours
rebuilding a system from scratch until it actually worked properly — like a
cross-platform course project I had to rework through several failed
approaches before landing on one that held up. It also can't show that a
real business is running offline-first software I built. That's the part a
portfolio proves that a resume just can't.
